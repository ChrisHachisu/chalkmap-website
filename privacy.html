Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ChalkMap (hereinafter referred to as the "App") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information. This policy is designed to align with international privacy standards including GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and general global best practices.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information (email address, username)
  • User-Generated Content (climbing logs, grades, photos, comments)
  • Location Data (GPS coordinates submitted by users)
  • Device and Usage Data (device type, operating system, analytics data)
  • Subscription and transaction data (if applicable)

3. How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Enable community features
  • Improve performance and user experience
  • Process subscriptions and payments
  • Communicate service-related notices
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse

4. Legal Basis for Processing (Where Applicable)

We process personal data based on user consent, performance of a contract (providing the App), compliance with legal obligations, and legitimate interests such as improving platform security.

5. Data Sharing

We may share data with trusted third-party service providers including hosting providers, analytics services, payment processors, and email service providers. We do not sell personal data.

6. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We take reasonable safeguards to protect data during international transfers.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the App and fulfill legal obligations. Users may request deletion of their data.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications

9. Security Measures

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact the App team through the official website or support email.

13. Location Data and Public Visibility

ChalkMap allows users to submit precise geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) for climbing areas, boulders, and parking locations.

  • Exact GPS coordinates are stored within the App's database.
  • If the associated content is made public, exact coordinates may be visible to other users.
  • Public users may copy and use such coordinates in external navigation applications.
  • ChalkMap does not collect background location tracking data or monitor user movement without active user submission.
  • Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to share geographic information and for respecting land access restrictions and applicable laws.

14. Public Content and Retention

User-submitted public content, including geographic coordinates, may remain available within the App as part of the community-driven database even if the original contributor deletes their account. Personal identifiers may be removed or anonymized upon account deletion.

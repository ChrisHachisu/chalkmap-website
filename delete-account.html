Delete Your Account

ChalkMap (developed by ChalkMap G.K.)

You can delete your ChalkMap account and its associated data at any time. This page explains how to request deletion and what happens to your data.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open the ChalkMap app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Open the menu and tap Settings.
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Your account is then scheduled for deletion. This action cannot be undone.

If you can't access the app

If you no longer have the app installed or cannot sign in, email support@chalkmap.com from the email address on your account and ask us to delete it. We will verify your identity and process the request.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove the personal data tied to it, including:

  • Your account and profile (name, username, email address, profile photo)
  • Your logged ascents (sends) and personal climbing data
  • Your private messages
  • Photos and content you added that have not been published to the shared community database

What data may be kept

Some information may be retained after account deletion:

  • Published community content. Crags, boulders, and problems you contributed and published to the shared database may be kept so the climbing information stays available to other climbers. Where possible this content is disassociated from your identity.
  • Records required by law. Limited records may be retained where required to meet legal, accounting, or security obligations, for as long as the law requires.

Questions

For any questions about deleting your account or data, contact support@chalkmap.com.
